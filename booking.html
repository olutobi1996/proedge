Book Your Sessions

ProEdge Coaching offers both 1-to-1 and small group football sessions designed to improve confidence, technique and overall performance in a professional and supportive environment.

Group sessions take place at Pond Lane Recreation Ground in Worthing (BN13 2RH), providing players with a great setting to train, develop and enjoy their football.

1-to-1 sessions are tailored to each individual player, focusing on key areas such as ball control, passing, dribbling and decision making to maximise development.

Pricing:
Group Sessions: £10 per child
1-to-1 Coaching: £40 per hour
Block Booking (5 sessions): £30 per session
