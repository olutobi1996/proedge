About Us

ProEdge Coaching was founded by Will Wood, a professional footballer with years of experience in the game, having developed through the academy system at Southampton before going on to play for clubs including Accrington Stanley, Dagenham & Redbridge, Ebbsfleet United, Dulwich Hamlet and Welling United.

Now currently playing for Havant & Waterlooville FC, Will has gained valuable experience across different levels of the professional game, developing a deep understanding of what it takes to perform consistently and compete at a high level.

Through his journey, he has worked with a range of coaches, styles and systems, allowing him to build strong technical knowledge, game awareness and professionalism. ProEdge Coaching is built around passing that experience on to the next generation, helping players improve, grow in confidence and reach their full potential.

Our Aim

At ProEdge Coaching, the aim is to create a professional and supportive environment where players can develop confidence, improve technically and enjoy their football.

Sessions are designed to replicate real match situations, focusing on key areas such as ball control, passing, decision making and movement both in and out of possession.

Training is delivered in small groups or 1-to-1, allowing for detailed coaching and individual feedback to support each player’s development.

Whether players are grassroots or academy level, the goal is to help them improve, build confidence and perform better in matches.
